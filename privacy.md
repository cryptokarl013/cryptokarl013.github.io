# Privacy Policy

This site — **cryptokarl013.xyz** — is a static research publication. It does not maintain user accounts, comment systems, or server-side forms. The following explains what limited data is collected when you visit.

## 1. Data collected by the site itself

**None directly.** The site is served as static HTML from GitHub Pages and does not run any server-side processing under the author's control. The author does not have access to any request logs.

## 2. Data collected by hosting (GitHub Pages)

The site is hosted on **GitHub Pages**. When you load any page, GitHub's servers log:

- Your IP address
- User agent (browser / OS)
- Referrer URL
- Timestamp of the request

These logs are controlled by GitHub and governed by [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement). The author of this site does not access, store, or process them.

## 3. Data collected by analytics (Google Analytics)

The site uses **Google Analytics 4** (measurement ID `G-C90F5LHLFF`) to understand aggregate readership. Google Analytics collects:

- Pseudonymous identifiers (a client-side cookie or similar token)
- Pages viewed, session duration, approximate geography (country/region)
- Referrer, device type, browser

Data is processed by Google under Google's Analytics terms. The author uses this data **only in aggregate** to understand which reports are being read and from which regions. The author does not export raw visitor data, attempt to re-identify visitors, or share analytics data with third parties.

If you prefer not to be measured:

- Install a browser extension such as **Google Analytics Opt-out**, **uBlock Origin**, or **Privacy Badger**.
- Use a browser with tracking protection enabled (Safari, Firefox strict mode).
- Enable Do Not Track in your browser — the site respects DNT signals where GA supports it.

## 4. Cookies

The site does not set first-party cookies beyond what the static theme template may emit. Google Analytics sets its own cookies (typically `_ga`, `_ga_<ID>`). No advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies are set by the author.

## 5. Third-party embedded content

Some reports embed or link to third-party services:

- **Arkham Intelligence** (intel.arkm.com) — address labels
- **Etherscan / Solscan** — block explorer links
- **Breadcrumbs.app** — fund-flow reports (linked, not iframed)
- **web.archive.org** — archived snapshots
- **External images from GitHub avatars / CDNs** (avatars.githubusercontent.com)

When you click a link to any of these services, the service's own privacy policy applies. The site does not embed iframes that would load third-party content directly into the page without your click.

## 6. Email contact

If you contact the author via **cryptokarl013@gmail.com**, your email is received by Google's Gmail service under Google's privacy terms. Emails are retained for as long as is reasonably useful for the correspondence (typically at least the statute-of-limitations period applicable to related reports). Emails are not published without your consent except as summarised — anonymously — for the purpose of recording that a correction request or right-of-reply was received.

## 7. Children

The site is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in applicable jurisdictions). If a child has nevertheless contacted the author, please advise so that the contact can be deleted.

## 8. Your rights (EU / UK / similar jurisdictions)

If you are a resident of a jurisdiction with data-protection law granting you rights over your personal data, you may:

- Request access to any personal data held about you (typically none, beyond any correspondence you have initiated).
- Request deletion of that data.
- Request that analytics data that includes your pseudonymous identifier be disassociated from future collection.

Send such requests to **cryptokarl013@gmail.com**.
