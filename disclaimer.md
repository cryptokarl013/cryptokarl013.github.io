# Legal Disclaimer

## What the content on this site is

- Transactions that have occurred on public blockchains and can be re-verified by any reader.

- Information published in public corporate registries (Companies House, ACRA, etc.) and regulatory press releases (OFAC, US Treasury, US Secret Service).

## What the content on this site is not

Reports on this site **are not**:

- **A judicial finding.** No claim published here has been adjudicated by a court. Reports describe behaviour; only a court can determine whether that behaviour is unlawful.

- **An allegation of criminal conduct by any named natural person.** Where natural persons are named in a report, the naming is limited to what appears in public filings. No statement on this site should be read as an allegation that a named person has committed a crime, unless the statement explicitly cites a public criminal proceeding or sanctions designation.
- **Investment, financial, legal, or tax advice.** Nothing on this site is a recommendation to buy, sell, hold, or avoid any asset. Reports are not a substitute for professional advice.
- **A real-time service.** Reports are snapshots at the time of publication. On-chain data changes continuously; external pages (LinkedIn profiles, Crunchbase entries) may be edited after a report is published. The report's `last_modified_at` date indicates the most recent review.

Where a report says "appears to", "is consistent with", "plausible that", or similar hedged language, the statement is the author's interpretation of the cited evidence, offered for the reader's own evaluation. It is not a statement of fact about intent.
